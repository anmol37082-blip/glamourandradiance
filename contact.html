Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How long does shipping take? +

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within the US. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available at checkout. International orders typically arrive within 10-14 business days.

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. If you are not completely satisfied, simply return the unused portion for a full refund. No questions asked!

Are your products suitable for sensitive skin? +

Yes! All our products are dermatologist-tested and formulated to be gentle on all skin types, including sensitive skin. We use only natural, hypoallergenic ingredients.

Do you offer international shipping? +

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on your location and order size.

How can I track my order? +

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section.

Are your products cruelty-free? +

Absolutely! We are Leaping Bunny certified and never test on animals. All our products are 100% cruelty-free and vegan-friendly.
