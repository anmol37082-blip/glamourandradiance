Our Story Begins With a Dream

Founded in 2018, Glamour & Radiance started as a small passion project in a tiny New York apartment. Our founder, Sophia Anderson, believed that everyone deserves access to high-quality, natural beauty products that actually work.

What began with just three handcrafted serums has now grown into a global brand trusted by over 100,000 customers worldwide. We remain committed to our founding principles: premium quality, natural ingredients, and affordable luxury.

Every product in our collection is carefully formulated with the finest botanical extracts, vitamins, and minerals. We never compromise on quality, and we never test on animals.

Sophia Anderson

Founder & CEO, Glamour & Radiance

Our Mission & Values

At Glamour & Radiance, we believe that true beauty comes from within. Our mission is to help every person discover their natural radiance through products that nourish, protect, and enhance their skin.

We are committed to sustainability, ethical sourcing, and transparency. Every ingredient we use is carefully selected for its efficacy and safety, ensuring that our customers receive only the best.
